Common mistakes to avoid when using food and drink guides

Also, don't forget to consider your own preferences and tastes - what works for someone else might not work for you!

Another mistake you should steer clear of is not doing your research before trusting a guide blindly. It's important to look into the credibility of the guide and see if it aligns with reputable sources or reviews.
